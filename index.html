What is Terra 159

Every county.
Every family.
One map.

Terra 159 is a food scarcity simulator built for all 159 counties in Georgia. Want to know what happens if a grocery store closes in your county? Which counties are food deserts — and which ones are food oases? It's all there, interactive and live.

This isn't a report. It's a tool. For policy people who need data and for moms who just need to find a farm stand.
